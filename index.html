📊 Diageo - Sales Report

This interactive Power BI dashboard analyzes sales and profit performance across products, cities, and stores. With dynamic filtering, users can explore business metrics, track trends, and gain insights to support decision-making.

🚀 Live Demo

❓ Business Questions Answered

  • Which cities and stores drive the highest revenue?
  • Which products deliver the best profit margins?
  • How do sales trends change throughout the year?
  • Where are the underperforming locations?
  • Are there seasonal patterns or spikes in demand?

🧩 Data Model

The data model follows a star schema, with a central fact table for sales metrics connected to dimension tables for products, stores, and time. The database was built in SQL Server, starting with well-structured dimension tables and foreign key relationships to the fact table. This ensured clean joins, relational integrity, and fast performance when loaded into Power BI.

📊 SUBE - Argentina’s Public Transport System dashboard

This Power BI dashboard visualizes public transport usage in Argentina using SUBE card data. It presents total trips across bus, train, and subway services, while allowing users to analyze trends by day, week, and month. The dashboard highlights usage patterns over time, the distribution of trips by transport type, and seasonal variations in passenger volume.

🚀 Live Demo

❓ Business Questions Answered

  • How many total trips are made by each type of transport?
  • What are the daily and monthly trends in public transport usage?
  • Which days of the week have the highest and lowest travel volumes?
  • How is ridership distributed among bus, train, and subway?
  • Are there seasonal peaks or changes in travel behavior across the year?
